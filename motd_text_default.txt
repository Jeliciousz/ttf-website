The Trans Fortress -------------
Hello! Welcome to The Trans Fortress! We're a small trans-focused queer community of TF2 players.
We accept all walks of life; queer or straight, human or critter!

Rules -------------
Must be 16 years old or older.
No bigotry or slurs, love everyone.
No NSFW or sexually explicit behavior/content.
Just don't be a grouch, really.

Other -------------
Use !rtv or "rtv" to try to Rock The Vote and change the map.
Use !scramble or just type "scramble" to start a vote for a team scramble. 
Use !nominate or "nominate" to bring up a menu of maps to add to the next vote. You can also use "!nominate [map name]" to skip the menu.
Please follow the rules and report any rule-breakers you see! We're trying to have nice things, here.<br>
